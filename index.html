ReplicationBench

We don't argue. We measure.

A catalog of accessible, amateur-completable kits for replicating contested, counterintuitive, and famous science experiments. Every one is built to a single standard, costs almost always under $2,000, and ships with the raw data from real runs — so you can check the result yourself instead of taking anyone's word for it. The conclusion is left to the measurement, not asserted by the maintainers.

The experiments

Each entry documents method and data, stated neutrally · status reflects the data, not anyone's expectations.
